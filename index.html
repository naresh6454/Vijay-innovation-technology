Our Journey

VIJAY INNOVATION TECHNOLOGY, an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, was established in the year 2022 by a professionally managed group of young, aspirant engineers.

Now, with the vision and objective of "MAKE IN INDIA CONCEPT" we are focusing on delivering products of global standards to various industrial segments.
